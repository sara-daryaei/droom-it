Case study

Bubble Paws

A warm, responsive website for a pet grooming service, designed to make services easy to understand and appointment requests easy to send.

View live website Start a similar project->
Client type
Pet grooming service
Role
UX/UI design, web design, development, deployment
Focus
Booking flow, contact capture, responsive service pages
Stack
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vercel, Neon

Challenge

Turn a simple service idea into a credible website visitors can act on.

Bubble Paws needed a clear digital presence for pet grooming services: a friendly first impression, service information, gallery/benefit pages, contact details and a way for visitors to request an appointment without friction.

01

Service-led structure

Navigation is organized around what visitors naturally look for: services, benefits, gallery and contact.

02

Booking-ready contact flow

The contact form includes service interest, preferred date and contact method, then saves the request to Neon.

03

Signup and data capture

A signup flow stores customer and pet details, creating a stronger foundation for future client management.

04

Deployment and email workflow

The project runs on Vercel with backend API routes and an automated Dutch appointment confirmation email.

Result

A portfolio-ready business website with real interaction behind the interface.

Bubble Paws demonstrates more than static page design: it shows responsive UI, contact conversion thinking, appointment capture, database integration and email automation. That makes it a strong example for future DROOM IT clients who need websites that actually support business workflows.

Business impact

Reducing the distance between interest and a real appointment request.

The project shows how a service website can guide visitors from a warm first impression to practical action: service understanding, preferred date selection, stored enquiry data and automated confirmation.
