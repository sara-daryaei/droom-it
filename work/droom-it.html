Case study

DROOM IT Website

A professional business website created to present DROOM IT as a web design, UX/UI and digital solutions partner for small businesses, startups and service brands.

View live website Start a similar project->
Client type
Web design and digital solutions studio
Role
Brand positioning, UX/UI design, web design, development
Focus
Credibility, conversion, bilingual audience, SEO structure
Stack
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vercel
Websites that make small businesses look ready for bigger clients.

Challenge

Move from a simple student-style website to a business-ready portfolio.

The website needed to communicate professional value quickly: what DROOM IT does, who it helps, why the work matters and how a potential client can start a project without confusion.

01

Stronger positioning

The homepage message was sharpened around business outcomes: trust, clarity, mobile usability and enquiries.

02

Agency-style UX

The page structure now includes proof, service paths, process, selected work, FAQ and clear project CTAs.

03

Portfolio depth

Case studies show not only design taste, but also how DROOM IT thinks about conversion and business workflows.

04

Launch and SEO foundation

The site is deployed on Vercel with responsive pages, metadata, sitemap entries and structured data for search engines.

Result

A stronger self-promotional website that can support client outreach.

DROOM IT now has a clearer portfolio experience for potential clients: a professional homepage, visible services, a strong work section and case studies that make the studio feel more credible and ready for real projects.
