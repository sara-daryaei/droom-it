Founder Story

DROOM IT is built for businesses that need their website to earn trust before the first conversation.

Sara Daryaei founded DROOM IT because many small businesses need more than a visually nice website. They need someone who listens carefully, takes responsibility and turns unclear ideas into a digital presence customers can understand and trust.

That is why every project is founder-led. Clients work directly with Sara from the first conversation until after launch, with clear communication, careful decisions and attention to the details that shape customer confidence.

Our Process

From first idea to a website that is ready to represent your business.

  1. 01Discover

    We understand your business, audience, current website and growth goals.

  2. 02Strategy

    We define the message, structure and customer path before designing screens.

  3. 03Design

    We create a premium visual direction with clear hierarchy and responsive layouts.

  4. 04Build

    We develop the website or application with performance, forms and integrations in mind.

  5. 05Launch

    We test, refine and prepare the site for a confident release.

  6. 06Grow

    We identify future improvements for SEO, conversion and customer workflows.
